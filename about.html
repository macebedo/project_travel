What is Diane's Travels all about?

Many people don't have time or knowledge to plan a trip before getting to their destination. Sometimes you can miss out on activities that require advanced booking, and why waste time on your vacation? If some things are taken care of ahead of time it leaves more time for fun!

Who is Diane?

Diane founded Diane's Travels after extensive trips around the globe. In her travels she felt unprepared and knew that with some advanced planning, vacations could be less stressful and filled with more activities she wanted. Knowing that others must have the same experience she founded Diane's Travels in 1991.

Why should I use Diane's Travels?

We'll service all your travel needs. Just gettings a cheap flight doesn't mean you'll have a great vacation. We start by figuring out what you want to do on vacation. Based on that info we can find a great place to stay, that's not only in your budget, but convenient to your activities. We prepare information and even book activities before your trip to make sure you have a great time.
