Popular Packages

Based on feedback from the many travelers we've served in the past, here are some highly recommended packages we think you'll enjoy. Of course we offer more packages and can custom tailor trips around your tastes and favorite activities.

Japan
Comprised of over 3,000 islands, Japan is a fascinating country. Of course Sushi lovers will be in heaven, but as the world's second largest economy, Japan has many cultural and natural attractions. While major cities like Tokyo are great, there are many places in Japan that you may want to see even if you have't heard their names before.

India
India is a vast, diverse country, from its people, to religions, clothes, dialects and more. You can experience jungle safaris, palace tours, camel rides, beaches, and so much more.
